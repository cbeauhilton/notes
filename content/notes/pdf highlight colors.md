-   🔴️ `Red` --> **Disagree With Author**
-   🟠️ `Orange` --> **Important Point By Author**
-   🟡️ `Yellow` --> **Interesting Point**
-   🟢️ `Green` --> **Important To Me**
-   🔵️ `Blue` --> **Notes After Initial Iteration**
-   🟣️ `Magenta` --> **literary note to look up later**
